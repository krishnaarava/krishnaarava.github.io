2Coolstuff Tips - Why Spend Money on Microsoft Office When You Have Openoffice

Feb 12th, 2010

Time and again many of my friends ask me these questions - Which Microsoft office version should they buy for their laptops? Which anti-virus to use?

So I want to share something many of them dont know. We have free version of office from Sun Microsystems (Oracle bought Sun Microsystems recently) that can be downloaded from Internet for free. Free is absolutely free, you don’t have to spend a penny! Still dont believe it?

Please download the latest Openoffice 3.2 from the below link.

http://download.openoffice.org/

The product is compatible with Microsoft Documents and is pretty good. It is available for all the operating systems Windows, Linux and Mac OS. The product is also supported with security updates and patches.

If you liked this tip, please do share it with your friends.
