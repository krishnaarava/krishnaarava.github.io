Create Deal Search Engine Using Google Reader

Jun 25th, 2009

Google Reader is great tool to read latest news and blog contents without visiting the websites. Basically its Web based RSS and Atom feed reader.

Most of the deals portals like woot.com, deals2buy.com, dealnews.com,slickdeals.net,amazon.com, etc support RSS.

To get started

1) Login into your Google Account at http://Google.com/reader (If you havent signed up yet for this service then you need to just agree to the terms of service and proceed)
2) Simply add all the deal related websites using “Add a subscription”
3) Organize all the deal related sites into an folder.

There you go - A search box is available use it to search for what ever you want.
